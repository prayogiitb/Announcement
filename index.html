Roundtable meet cum workshop on “Challenges and Trends in Water Quality Monitoring of Rivers".

The Centre for Technology Alternatives for Rural Areas (CTARA) at IIT Bombay, in collaboration with experts from the University of Vermont (UofV), Burlington, USA, and University of Toronto (UofT), Canada, is arranging a roundtable meet cum workshop on “Challenges and Trends in Water Quality Monitoring of Rivers” in February 2024. The agenda of the meeting is to bring the various stakeholders pertaining to the water quality monitoring of rivers together including members from Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB), Karnataka Pollution Control Board (KPCB), Water Supply and Sanitation Department of Maharashtra, etc. The aim is to understand the practices and challenges while forming and implementing the policies to monitor and protect the river water quality.

To attend the event contact us on- meghrajgarad3713@iitbombay.org

*Entries are limited and restricted to resource persons/stakeholders practicing/working in the surface water quality monitoring, river actions plans field, academicians working on designing and planning water quality monitoring network.

Date of Workshop: 21st and 22nd February 2024
Venue: VMCC, IIT BOMBAY
