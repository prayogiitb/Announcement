Launch of 1-year online program - 'Master in Development Practice' by IIT-Bombay

The ‘new academic program’ titled 'Master in Development Practice' is targeted to professionally equip young development practitioners having some field-experience, by building their academic and analytical abilities as well as the professional skills required for becoming an effective and efficient practice-professional in the development sector.

Start Date/Time:24 March 2023 - 18 May 2023
Venue: CTARA IIT-Bombay
